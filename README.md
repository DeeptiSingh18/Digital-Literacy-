# Digital-Literacy
In today's time, technology has become a very important part of our daily life. We use digital tools for studying, communication, and even for entertainment. Because of this, it is necesaary to understand how to use these tools properly and responsibly.This project helped me explore different aspects of the digital world in a practical way.
In this project, I worked on various tasks like creating an infographic, building my digital portfolio, solving coding problems on hackerrank, learning how to write professional emails, and understanding cybercrime through a case study. Each task was different and helped me lean something new.

## Objectives
1) To understand the importance of digital literacy in modern life.
2) To develop skills in visua communication and content design.
3) To gain hands-on experience with online platforms and tools.
4) To improve professional communication through email etiquette.
5) To build awareness about cybercrime and online safety.
6) To create a structured and presentable digital portfolio.
